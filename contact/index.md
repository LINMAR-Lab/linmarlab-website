# {% include icon.html icon="fa-regular fa-envelope" %} Contact

Understanding how biological systems evolve, interact, and adapt.

Our research group is based at the **Faculty of Science of the University of Málaga (Spain)** and brings together researchers from the **Department of Botany and Plant Physiology** and the **Department of Molecular Biology and Biochemistry**. We combine **experimental approaches, microbial ecology, and theoretical and computational modelling** to understand the mechanisms that shape **adaptation, ecological interactions, and evolutionary dynamics** across biological systems. We are always open to **new ideas, interdisciplinary collaborations, and emerging research directions**, and we welcome students and researchers interested in exploring these topics with us.
